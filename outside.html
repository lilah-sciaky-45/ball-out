Who Can Play

Pickup for queer women, trans, non-binary, gender expansive ballers and allies. Skill level: Intermediate (think intermural or high school). Show up solo or with friends. We play half court 4v4 and full court 5v5.

What To Bring

Yourself, water, and a size 6 ball if you have one. We have pennies.

Cost

Free when outdoors. $5 when at Charles Jordan Community Center in the rain.
