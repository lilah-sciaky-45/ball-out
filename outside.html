Who Can Play

Pickup for queer, trans, women, non-binary, and ally ballers. All skill levels welcome. Show up solo or with friends — you'll get runs.

What To Bring

Yourself, sneakers, and water. We play with a Size 6 ball. Winner stays on.

Cost

Free. Always. This is community ball.
